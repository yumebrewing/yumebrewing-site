About Yume

Yume means dream.

Yume Brewing Co. was created with a simple idea: great beer belongs in great moments. Inspired by the Japanese word for dream, Yume is a light lager brewed with a subtle lift of yuzu citrus. Fresh, crisp, and endlessly drinkable - it’s made for long evenings, good food, and the moments you wish would last a little longer.

Why Yuzu

Yuzu is a prized Japanese citrus known for its bright aroma and delicate complexity.

Unlike lemon or lime, yuzu adds a delicate aromatic freshness that complements the clean body of a light lager without overpowering it.

The result is a crisp, refreshing beer designed for long summer evenings, shared tables, and modern menus.

  • Subtle citrus aroma
  • Light, refreshing finish
  • Designed for food pairing
  • Perfect for warm climates
