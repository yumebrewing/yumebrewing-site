The Beer

Yume is a bright, clean light lager brewed with a subtle yuzu finish. It is designed for all-day drinkability with a crisp character that pairs naturally with modern food menus.

  • Style: Light Lager
  • Flavour: Citrus lift from Japanese yuzu
  • Finish: Crisp and refreshing
  • Serve: Best enjoyed ice cold
