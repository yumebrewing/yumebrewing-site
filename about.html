About

Our Story

Yume Brewing Co. was created with a simple ambition: to brew a lager that feels perfectly at home on modern tables.

Inspired by the shared drinking traditions found across Asia and Africa where drinks are meant to be enjoyed together over food and conversation Yume is brewed in Cape Town and subtly lifted with Japanese yuzu, bringing a fresh perspective to one of the world’s most classic beer styles.

Clean, crisp, and endlessly drinkable, it’s designed for good company, great meals, and the moments that naturally last a little longer than planned.

What Yume Means

Yume (pronounced you-meh) means dream in Japanese.

The name reflects the moments the beer is made for sunny afternoons, long evenings, shared tables, and the simple pleasure of a well-made lager enjoyed in good company.

Because some of the best moments aren’t planned they happen when good food, good drink, and the right company come together.

Brand Philosophy

Yume is designed to feel modern, refreshing, and food-friendly. The profile draws from subtle Asian flavour inspiration, balanced with restraint so the beer stays crisp and approachable.

Founder Perspective

Built from a Chinese South African perspective, Yume reflects an understanding of hospitality, drinks culture, and the kind of product contemporary venues want to serve with confidence.
